Formerly known as MonoKey, JANELS is a dynamic musician who has evolved from being a celebrated DJ to a multifaceted music producer and songwriter. After years of reading the crowd and absorbing different musical influences, his tracks are a fusion of electronic beats, organic instrumentation, and exotic sounds. Creating a unique blend of genres.

Born and raised in Germany, JANELS has always been fascinated by the diverse sounds and rhythms of the world. Drawing from this, his music is a reflection of the beauty and diversity of cultures. With several upcoming releases in the pipeline, JANELS will take you on a journey of discovery, exploring new music that will leave you wanting more.
